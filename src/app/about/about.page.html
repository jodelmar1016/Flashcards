Study Buddy

Was developed by BSCS in fulfillment of their course Mobile Computing 2.

Study Buddy is a user-friendly flashcard application. It is a type of reviewer that lets users create a relevant topic for their subject matter, which enhances their writing and cognitive skills. It has two main features: creating cards for reviewers and testing your knowledge through quizzes.
